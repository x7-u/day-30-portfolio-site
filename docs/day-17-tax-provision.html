About the project

UK corporation tax computation with deferred tax, R&D identification, and effective rate calculation.

Local-only Flask web app and CLI that turns accounting profit into a defensible UK corporation tax provision: book-to-tax reconciliation, marginal-relief banding, deferred tax on PPE timing differences, and an AI scan over the business's activities that flags potential R&D tax-credit opportunities with verbatim citations.

At a glance

Standard
UK CT 2026 (small 19% / main 25% / MRR 3-200), IAS 12 deferred tax
Inputs
PBT + add-backs + CAs + losses + FII + PPE NBV / TWDV + opening deferred tax
Outputs
Current tax + deferred tax + ETR + CT600 box reconciliation + R&D scan
Model
DeepSeek V4, single call (~$0.0005 per scan)

How it works

  • Tax schema. tax_schema.py wraps the inputs in a typed TaxInput dataclass.
  • Corporation tax maths. tax_maths.compute runs the CT600 walk. PBT plus add-backs (depreciation, amortisation, disallowable) gives adjusted profit.
  • AI R&D scan. rd_identifier.scan asks DeepSeek V4 to identify R&D-qualifying projects in the user's plain-English activities description, keyed to HMRC BEIS criteria (advance in science /...
  • GOV.UK / HMRC UI. Distinct from every prior day's palette family (Bloomberg, orbs, parchment, magazine, FT pink, engineering, Bauhaus, textbook, warehouse hi-vis, dark cocoa).
  • Excel + CSV exports. openpyxl writes a 6-sheet workbook (Summary with embedded chart PNGs + Reconciliation + Rate band + Deferred tax + R&D scan + Inputs).

AI integration

DeepSeek flags R&D eligible spend

Limitations

  • Single-period view. No multi-period rollforward.
  • PPE only for deferred tax. Provisions, share schemes, intangibles, pensions and tax losses are out of scope.
  • No group relief. Associated companies count divides the thresholds, but inter-company loss surrender is not modelled.
  • No quarterly instalment payments. Headline current-tax charge only; cash-flow implications of QIPs / large-company status are post-30.
  • Capital allowances at user level. User supplies a single capital_allowances figure; AIA / FYA / pools / full-expensing allocation is computed externally.
  • AI proposes, does not opine on qualification.

Fun facts

  • A UK corporation tax computation, deferred tax and all, that also points out spend which might qualify for R&D relief.
  • It works out the effective tax rate for you too.

Walk-through

  1. Cold start - GOV.UK / HMRC tax-return aesthetic: white page, near-black GOV.UK body text, official HMRC green for primary actions,...
  2. Compose region with sample loaded - CT600 box references in the field gutter. Heavy 2px borders.
  3. Headline ribbon - Four monolithic cells: TTP / current tax / deferred tax P&L charge / total tax.
  4. Schedule I: book-to-tax reconciliation - Every CT600 line stamped with its box number.
  5. Schedule II: rate band - Lower / upper limits divided by (associated + 1), augmented profits, marginal relief computation step by step.
  6. Schedule III: deferred tax - Timing-difference approach. Closing balance = (PPE NBV less PPE TWDV) at the measurement rate.
