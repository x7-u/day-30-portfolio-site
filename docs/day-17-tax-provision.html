About the project

UK corporation tax computation with deferred tax, R&D identification, and effective rate calculation.

Local-only Flask web app and CLI that turns accounting profit into a defensible UK corporation tax provision: book-to-tax reconciliation, marginal-relief banding, deferred tax on PPE timing differences, and an AI scan over the business's activities that flags potential R&D tax-credit opportunities with verbatim citations.

Fun facts
