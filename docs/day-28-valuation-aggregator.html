About the project

Four valuation methods side by side (DCF, EV/EBITDA, P/E, NAV) with AI weighting by sector and stage.

A local Flask app and CLI that runs four valuation methods side by side (DCF, EV/EBITDA, P/E, NAV), draws the football-field chart, and lets DeepSeek V4 weight the methods based on sector and stage and write the valuation conclusion.

At a glance

Use case
Investment-bank pitch valuation, M&A football-field
Inputs
Financials + peer multiples + DCF inputs + sector
Outputs
4-method football field + AI weighted central + 3 CSVs + Excel + 3 charts
Model
Single DeepSeek call (~$0.0004 per pitch)

How it works

  • Four methods, four perspectives. DCF: 5-year EBITDA forecast at the forecast growth rate, discounted at WACC, plus a Gordon terminal at the terminal growth rate.
  • Bands and football field. Each method emits a low / central / high estimate using +/- 15 to 30 percent bands.
  • AI weighting matrix. valuation_ai.commentary uses a sector x method matrix.
  • Pitch-deck UI. Distinct from every prior day's palette.
  • Excel + CSV exports. openpyxl writes the multi-sheet workbook (Summary with embedded charts and gold-coloured weighted central, Methods detail, Commentary typeset, Inputs).

AI integration

DeepSeek weights methods + writes conclusion

Limitations

  • Comps multiples are user-supplied. Live market data integration is post-30.
  • Single-currency. Cross-border valuation in mixed currencies is post-30.
  • DCF is EBITDA-proxy. True free-cash-flow build (capex, change in working capital, tax) is in Day 9.
  • AI weights are advisory. The analyst signs off on the weighting before the valuation goes into a pitch deck.
  • Not investment advice. For educational use.

Fun facts

  • It lines up four ways of valuing a company side by side (DCF, multiples, P/E, and net assets) in the classic 'football field' chart bankers love.
  • One picture, four opinions, a sensible conclusion.

Walk-through

  1. Home page (cold start) - Investment bank pitch deck aesthetic. Slate dark sections, gold and orange accents, deep navy slate.
  2. First data run: capital-light SaaS - Tech_saas sample. Velocity Cloud plc.
  3. Second data run: REIT - Asset_heavy sample. Riverside Property plc.
  4. Figures tab (what the Excel export embeds) - Three charts: the football field with diamonds and a market-price reference line, the method centrals as side-by-side...
  5. AI commentary (the integration) - DeepSeek V4 reads the digest and writes the headline, method weights, per-method notes, valuation conclusion, and risks...
