Sentiment Pulse

Financial news sentiment with sector aggregation
STACKPython / Flask / matplotlib / DeepSeek FOCUSMarket context for investment analysis PORT:1005 TESTS65

About the project

Ingests news headlines; classifies sentiment per company and rolls up to sector. Bias-aware aggregation, RAG flags on extreme movements.

Local-only Flask web app and CLI for scoring company news against price action with DeepSeek V4. Drag in a workbook of headlines + a price series, get back a buy-side daily brief with per-article sentiment, a one-line investment implication for each, and an analyst summary on the cycle.

At a glance

Model
DeepSeek V4 (deepseek-chat default)
Cost
around $0.0024 per workbook of 20 articles

AI integration

DeepSeek classifies headline sentiment

Fun facts

  • It reads a wall of financial news headlines and rolls the mood up from single companies to whole sectors.
  • Extreme swings in sentiment get a traffic-light flag, so the signal stands out from the noise.
