About the project

Compute break-even volume and revenue from fixed costs, variable costs and price. Monte Carlo overlay shows the probability distribution of profit.

Local-only Flask app and CLI for break-even analysis with sensitivity bands and an AI verdict on whether the cost structure works.

At a glance

Model
DeepSeek V4 (deepseek-chat default), one verdict call + optional follow-up
Cost
around $0.0001 per analysis.

How it works

  • The Tool. Pure-Python deterministic core. cvp_schema.py parses the workbook into typed dataclasses (CVPMetadata, CVPInputs, Product, StepCost, VolumePoint).
  • AI Verdict + Follow-up. One DeepSeek V4 call per analysis. The system prompt instructs a CFO voice, two to three sentences, do-not-restate the inputs.
  • Excel & Exports. openpyxl writes a 3-sheet workbook (Summary, Inputs, Sensitivity) with embedded break-even chart and tornado PNGs (rendered by matplotlib, Agg backend).

AI integration

DeepSeek interprets break-even output

Fun facts

  • It finds the exact point where a business stops losing money and starts making it, then runs thousands of simulations to show how likely you are to get there.
  • Cost-volume-profit analysis with a Monte Carlo twist: the answer is a probability, not just a single number.
