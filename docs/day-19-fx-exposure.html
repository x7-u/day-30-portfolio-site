About the project

Aggregate FX exposure by currency and tenor; AI recommends hedging instruments and ratios.

Local Flask app + CLI for treasury-style FX exposure reporting. Drop in a multi-currency AR/AP ledger plus spot rates; the engine converts everything to GBP, nets per currency, flags material positions, stress-tests a 5% and 10% adverse move, and DeepSeek V4 recommends a hedge instrument per position (forward / natural hedge / currency account / stay open).

AI integration

DeepSeek recommends hedging strategy

Fun facts

  • It nets off your currency exposure by currency and time horizon, then suggests how much to hedge and with what.
  • Treasury-desk thinking, made approachable.
