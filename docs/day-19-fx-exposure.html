FX Exposure

FX exposure analysis with hedging recommendations
STACKPython / Flask / matplotlib / DeepSeek FOCUSFX risk management PORT:1019 TESTS32

About the project

Aggregate FX exposure by currency and tenor; AI recommends hedging instruments and ratios.

Local Flask app + CLI for treasury-style FX exposure reporting. Drop in a multi-currency AR/AP ledger plus spot rates; the engine converts everything to GBP, nets per currency, flags material positions, stress-tests a 5% and 10% adverse move, and DeepSeek V4 recommends a hedge instrument per position (forward / natural hedge / currency account / stay open).

At a glance

Standard
IAS 21 (FX translation), IFRS 7 (financial risk disclosures)
Inputs
Multi-currency AR/AP ledger + spot rates + material threshold
Outputs
Per-currency net position + material flags + 5/10% stress + AI hedge recommendations
Model
DeepSeek V4, single call (~$0.001 per analysis)

How it works

  • FX schema. fx_schema.py wraps the inputs in a typed FxInput dataclass with FxEntry sub-records.
  • FX maths. fx_maths.compute converts every ledger line to GBP at the supplied rate, groups by currency, separates AR from AP, and computes net per currency.
  • AI hedge recommender. hedging_ai.recommend builds a digest (total AR/AP/net, per-currency positions with direction, materiality, and sensitivity) and asks DeepSeek V4 for JSON: overall_summary,...
  • Midnight FX desk UI. Distinct from every prior day's palette.
  • Excel + CSV exports. openpyxl writes a 5-sheet workbook (Summary with embedded charts + Positions + Entries (GBP converted) + Hedges + Inputs).

AI integration

DeepSeek recommends hedging strategy

Limitations

  • Rates are user-supplied. No live FX feed.
  • Spot only. Forward rates would need a yield-curve input.
  • Worst-case stress only. 5% and 10% adverse moves are symmetric absolute-value stresses; correlated multi-currency moves (e.g., USD up + EUR down at once) are not modelled.
  • No hedge accounting under IFRS 9. Designation, effectiveness testing, and the cash-flow vs fair-value hedge distinction are out of scope.
  • GBP base only. Multi-base support (USD or EUR as base for international subsidiaries) is post-30.
  • AI proposes, does not execute. Recommendations are starting points for a treasurer; actual hedge execution requires a counterparty bank and treasury policy sign-off.

Fun facts

  • It nets off your currency exposure by currency and time horizon, then suggests how much to hedge and with what.
  • Treasury-desk thinking, made approachable.
