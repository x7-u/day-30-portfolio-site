About the project

Drop in an earnings call transcript; AI extracts the forward-looking statements, hedge phrases, and claims with confidence scoring.

Local-only Flask app and CLI for buy-side earnings call analysis. Drag in a transcript (.txt or .pdf), or paste it. The tool detects speaker turns, infers roles (CEO / CFO / EXEC / ANALYST / OPERATOR), runs a deterministic hedge / certainty / deflection rule detector and a quantitative claim regex, makes one DeepSeek V4 call to extract guidance + themes + Q&A + risks + a bull/bear verdict, and renders it all in a Bloomberg-terminal aesthetic.

AI integration

DeepSeek extracts hedge phrases and claims

Fun facts

  • Feed it an earnings call transcript and it fishes out the hedge phrases and forward-looking promises management would rather you skimmed past.
  • Every claim it extracts comes with a confidence score.
