About the project

A web-based backtesting engine that runs Pine Script v5 and MQL5 Expert Advisor strategies against your own OHLCV data, or data fetched straight from MetaTrader 5. A dual interpreter with Cython acceleration handles multi-timeframe aggregation, walk-forward optimisation and risk-based position sizing, and reports 45+ performance metrics. It also simulates funded-account evaluation phases and includes a deep data-analysis suite.

How it works

  • Dual engine. One app runs both Pine Script v5 and MQL5 EA strategies, with a Cython-accelerated core for speed.
  • Multi-timeframe. Higher timeframes are auto-aggregated from the base data, with spread, slippage, commission and intra-bar stop/target priority modelled.
  • Walk-forward. Parameter optimisation runs in parallel across walk-forward windows.
  • Funded simulation. Simulates prop-firm phase 1 and phase 2 evaluations, with daily drawdown enforcement and a pass timeline.
  • Data analysis. Equity-curve overlays, a 7x24 hour-by-day trade heatmap, a consecutive-loss simulator and drawdown-recovery charts.
  • Position sizing. Risk-based lot sizing from stop distance and equity, with currency-aware profit conversion across pairs.

Limitations

  • A backtesting and analysis tool, not a live trading bot.
  • MetaTrader 5 data fetching needs a local MT5 install.
