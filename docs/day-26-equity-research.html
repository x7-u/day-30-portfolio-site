About the project

Drop in five years of financials; AI writes the rating, target price, investment thesis, bull and bear cases, risks and catalysts.

A small Flask app and CLI that takes a stock ticker plus a financial history and produces a one-page sell-side equity research note. The engine computes the multiples and the growth and margin signals; DeepSeek V4 writes the rating, target, thesis, bull and bear cases, and the risks and catalysts list.

Fun facts

  • It writes a one-page sell-side research note (rating, target price, the bull case and the bear case) from five years of financials.
  • The sort of note that takes an analyst a day, drafted in a moment.
