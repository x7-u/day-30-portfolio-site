About the project

Describe a business in plain English; AI designs the chart of accounts for entity type, industry, size and standard. Validated against the 1000-9999 convention.

A small Flask app and CLI that takes a plain-English business description and generates a complete, structured chart of accounts appropriate for the entity type, industry, size and accounting standard. DeepSeek V4 designs the COA; a rules-based validator confirms uniqueness, code ranges and normal balances.

Fun facts
